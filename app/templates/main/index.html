Microgreens

Microgreens are vegetables and herbs that are harvested while still in the seedling stage. At that point, they can have up 40 times the nutrients of the mature plant and are loaded with antioxidants and other beneficial nutrients.

Microgreens can generally be used in dishes instead of the mature plant. They also make a great garnish, are good in salads, and add a pop of flavor, texture, and color to any dish.

Currently, we offer the following varieties of microgreens:

  • Broccoli - Broccoli microgreens are tender and have a muted broccoli flavor.
  • Pea - Sweet and stringy. These greens produce tendrils, making them visually appealing as well as delicious.
  • Radish - Spicy and colorful, just like the mature form. This makes a great accent to any dish. They can also be used in salads or anywhere else you would use radishes.
  • Sunflower - These microgreens are crunchy and provide a nutty flavor. These even go great on peanut butter and jelly sandwhiches!

Eggs

Quail eggs are a delicious nutrient-rich egg that comes in a small package. We collect them daily from our small backyard flock. These taste just like chicken eggs and you can use four of them in place of a large chicken egg in recipes.

Houseplants

Houseplants can help reduce stress and liven up your living space. We have a range of houseplants from vining plants to herbs, but we do have limited stock. Please contact us for details on what we have available at the moment.

Here are the plants we typically carry:

  • Pothos - A vining plant that handles low-light well. We have the following varieties.
    • Golden - A varegated variety displaying golden streaks across its foliage.
    • Marble - A varegated variety displaying pale white and green speckling.
    • Jade - The classic dark green vining plant.
  • Spider Plant - Spider plants are a tropical grass that produces offspring via hangning plant babies, making it look like a spider. This plant is great in hanging baskets or on shelf or plant stand.
  • Flaming Katy - A beautiful succulent plant that produces bright red flowers when given enough light.
  • English Ivy - A beautiful low light sprawling ivy. This is another great plant for hanging baskets.
  • Herbs - We usually have plenty of herbs that make great indoor houseplants as well. We reccomend keeping these ones near the kitchen for visual appeal and fresh seasoning.
    • Peppermint
    • Spearmint
    • Apple Mint (Fuzzy Mint)
    • Chocolate Mint
    • Lemon Balm
    • Rosemary
    • Oregano
    • Sage
    • Thyme
    • Basil
    • Lemon Basil

Terrariums

Terrariums are nature in a bottle. Inside of each terrariums is a self contained ecosystem, with plants, a water-cycle, and even small organisms that clean up the waste. All of our terrariums are hand-crafted and one of a kind. They are built inside of recycled containers (mostly whiskey and rum bottles).
